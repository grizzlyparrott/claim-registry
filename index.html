Claim Registry

A neutral, deterministic method for turning a text claim and optional attachments into a tamper-evident evidence packet that can be verified offline.

Public spec Verification walkthrough Sample packets
Reference implementation is open source and runs locally. No backend required.

What this is

Each recorded item is a JSON record plus optional files, packaged with a manifest of SHA-256 hashes. Records are also appended to a JSONL log that forms a hash chain, so edits or deletions become obvious.

What it is not

It is not a legal opinion, a timestamp authority, or a guarantee that a claim is true. It is a reproducible way to preserve what was observed and detect tampering after the fact.
